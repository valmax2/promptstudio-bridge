```
Poly Reducer 3D
```

**Short description** (max 80)
```
Reduce polygons of STL and OBJ 3D files on your phone, without losing the shape.
```

**Full description** (max 4000)
```
Poly Reducer 3D shrinks your 3D models right on your phone.

Open an STL or OBJ file, move the slider and cut the triangle count while
keeping the model's shape. Great for 3D printing, sharing lighter files, or
preparing models for apps and games.

FEATURES
• Open .STL and .OBJ files (ASCII and binary)
• High-quality polygon reduction (edge-collapse) with real-time preview
• See original / current triangle count and estimated file size instantly
• Export back to STL or OBJ
• Display modes: wireframe, flat-shaded, smooth
• Rotate and orient the model: by finger or by exact degrees (X/Y/Z)
• Fixed views (front, back, side, top, bottom) and turntable auto-rotation
• iOS-style interface, light & dark themes, custom palettes
• Works offline

No account, no hassle: open, reduce, export.

Keywords: STL, OBJ, reduce polygons, mesh decimation, optimize mesh,
3D printing, lighten 3D model, reduce triangles, 3D viewer.
```

## Materiali grafici richiesti da Play
- **Icona**: 512×512 (Play la genera dall'app; l'icona sorgente è in `assets/`).
- **Feature graphic**: 1024×500 (banner in alto nella scheda) — obbligatoria.
- **Screenshot telefono**: min. 2 (consigliati 4–6). Idee efficaci:
  1. Modello **prima** (tanti triangoli) vs **dopo** (ridotto) — mostra il numero.
  2. Lo **slider** in azione con l'anteprima.
  3. Modalità **filo di ferro** (fa capire "poligoni").
  4. Menu **Impostazioni** (temi/colori) per far vedere la cura dell'app.
  5. **Export** con la dimensione del file ridotta.
- Consiglio: aggiungi una didascalia breve su ogni screenshot ("Riduci del 70%",
  "Anteprima in tempo reale", "Esporta STL/OBJ").
